AIRLINE PROFITABILITY ANS SALES DUBAI (INTERACTIVE DASHBOARD IN EXCEL)

A comprehensive analytics project focused on evaluating financial performance, route profitability, fleet utilization, and seasonal travel demand for Dubai’s aviation operations.
kaggle link : https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/waleedfaheem/airline-route-profitability-and-cost-analysis

Project Overview
The Dubai aviation hub is a pivotal center for global passenger transit and international trade. This project leverages a dataset of 7,975 flight records to analyze profitability trends, aircraft performance, and seasonal demand patterns.
By using interactive pivot tables, dynamic slicers, and key performance indicators (KPIs), this dashboard translates raw flight data into actionable business strategies

📊 Key Business Insights & Takeaways
High-Margin Fleet Performance: Specific aircraft models (e.g., Boeing 777-300ER and Airbus A380) drive the majority of total profit and maintain strong seat load factors.
Route vs. Cost Balance: High total revenue on certain routes does not always equal top profitability due to high operational costs.
Seasonal Demand Shifts: Revenue fluctuates based on travel seasons (Shoulder, Peak, Low, and Normal periods), requiring tailored pricing strategies.
Ancillary Revenue Stream: Services such as baggage fees, seat upgrades, and onboard meals provide a stable, supplementary revenue stream parallel to ticket sales.
